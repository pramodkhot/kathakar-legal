KathaKar — Delete Account

We respect your right to delete your account and all associated data from KathaKar.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1 — Email Request (Recommended):

  1. Send an email to pramodsk1214@gmail.com
  2. Use subject line: KathaKar Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address associated with your KathaKar account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days

What Data Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, email, photo)
  • Your reading history and bookmarks
  • Your library and saved stories
  • Your comments and ratings
  • Your coin balance and transaction history

What Data May Be Retained

Note: Stories and poems you have published will be removed from public view but may be retained in our backup systems for up to 90 days before permanent deletion.

If you are an author with active readers, we recommend marking your stories as drafts before requesting deletion.
